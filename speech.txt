
  After 2 months of studies of this language I've developed these thoughts.

1. It is important for me to be the best version of me by taking as example other universes, other results from all other people I should have known (and I will).
2. I need to know more cultures, and for this I am ready collaborate with more people (and respecting them).
3. I act for personal gain (for my job/field/work) but also I would like to create a team/friendships with very special individuals/talents.
4. To survive we should assemble a new "intelligence", beyond Europe I think we (special persons) need a new strong International cooperation.
